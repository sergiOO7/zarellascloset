About

Zarella's Closet

Zarella's Closet, a delightful local business in Calgary, Alberta, specializing in handcrafted knitting products, was founded over a decade ago by a dynamic mother-daughter duo. Born from their shared passion for the art of knitting, this family-owned company is committed to quality and craftsmanship. From cozy blankets to trendy hats, each item is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans. Zarella's Closet embraces the community spirit, sourcing materials locally and offering a warm, inviting storefront for knitting enthusiasts. It's more than a craft company; it's a celebration of tradition, creativity, and the joy of handmade warmth in the heart of Calgary.
